VAST-Ghana Media Awards

Celebrating Excellence in Health Journalism and Sustainable Development Reporting

The VAST-Ghana Media Awards recognize outstanding journalists and media practitioners who demonstrate exceptional commitment to health journalism, sustainable development reporting, and advocacy for social justice. These awards celebrate the power of media to drive positive change in Ghanaian society.

Health Reporting Excellence

Awarded to journalists who demonstrate exceptional coverage of health issues, including disease prevention, health promotion, and healthcare access in Ghana.

Climate & Health Journalism

Recognizing outstanding reporting on the intersection of climate change and public health, highlighting environmental justice issues affecting Ghanaian communities.

Community Health Advocacy

Celebrating media initiatives that effectively amplify community voices and drive policy change in health and sustainable development.
