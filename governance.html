Governance

Committed to Excellence in Governance

Transparent, Accountable, and Effective Leadership

VAST-Ghana operates under a robust governance framework that ensures transparency, accountability, and strategic direction. Our governance structure is designed to maximize our impact while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and ethical conduct.

Strategic Leadership and Oversight

Mr. Kwame Mensah

Governance Structure

Our Organizational Framework

General Assembly

Highest decision-making body comprising all members

↓

Board of Directors

Strategic oversight and governance (6 members)

↓

Executive Management

Daily operations and program implementation

↓

Program Teams

Specialized teams for research, advocacy, and community programs

Board Committees

Specialized Oversight and Advisory Functions

Finance & Audit Committee

Oversight of financial management, budgeting, and audit processes. Ensures transparency and accountability in all financial matters.

Programs & Impact Committee

Monitors program effectiveness, impact measurement, and strategic alignment with organizational goals.

Governance & Nominations Committee

Board development, governance best practices, and leadership succession planning.
