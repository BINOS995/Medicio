Welcome to VAST-Ghana

The Vision for Accelerated Sustainable Development, Ghana (VAST-Ghana) is a registered civil society organization based in Accra, dedicated to promoting sustainable health and development through evidence-informed advocacy, health promotion, social justice, community empowerment, and inclusive participation in Ghana and beyond.

    About VAST-Ghana

    Vision for Accelerated Sustainable Development, Ghana

    Mission and Vision

    VAST-Ghana is committed to transforming health outcomes across Ghana through sustainable development initiatives.

    • Mission: To promote sustainable health by mobilizing, informing, and empowering individuals through health promotion, research, and advocacy.
    • Vision: To create a society where all people enjoy optimal health standards.
    • Approach: Evidence-informed advocacy, health promotion, social justice, community empowerment, and inclusive participation.

    Based in Accra, Ghana, we work tirelessly to ensure that health policies and practices serve the needs of all communities, particularly the most vulnerable populations.

    Recent Initiatives

    Our latest advocacy efforts and achievements in promoting sustainable health

    WHO World No Tobacco Award 2025

    Mr. Labram Musah, the Executive Director of VAST-Ghana, received the prestigious WHO World No Tobacco Award in recognition of his outstanding efforts in tobacco control.

    Advocacy for Higher Excise Taxes

    VAST-Ghana has strongly advised the Ministry of Finance to uphold and potentially increase existing excise taxes on tobacco, alcohol, and sugar-sweetened beverages to combat non-communicable diseases.

    Youth Protection Initiative

    Backed the National Youth Authority CEO's call for a nationwide ban on electronic cigarettes, shisha, and other tobacco products targeting the youth to protect young people from health-harming substances.
