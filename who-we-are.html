Who We Are

Vision for Alternative Development

Vision for Accelerated Sustainable Development Ghana is a non-governmental organization committed to the development of alternatives to tobacco growing and the improvement of public health in Ghana. We envision a society where tobacco production is replaced with sustainable, healthy alternatives that benefit both farmers and communities.

Our Core Identity

As a leading public health advocacy organization in Ghana, we serve as a bridge between policy makers, farmers, health professionals, and communities. Our work is grounded in evidence-based research, community engagement, and policy advocacy to create lasting change in tobacco control and sustainable development.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Upholding transparency and accountability in all our endeavors
  • Evidence-based: Using scientific research to guide our interventions
  • Community-centered: Placing communities at the heart of our work
  • Sustainability: Promoting long-term solutions for health and development
  • Collaboration: Working with diverse stakeholders for maximum impact
