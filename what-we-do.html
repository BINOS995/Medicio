What We Do

Comprehensive Public Health Solutions

Transforming Communities Through Strategic Interventions

We implement multi-faceted programs that address public health challenges through research, advocacy, community engagement, and policy development. Our work spans from grassroots community interventions to national policy influence.

Tobacco Control & Prevention

Leading comprehensive tobacco control initiatives including policy advocacy, public education, and cessation support programs.

  • Policy advocacy for tobacco control legislation
  • Public awareness campaigns on tobacco harms
  • Smoking cessation programs and support
  • Monitoring tobacco industry interference
  • Enforcement of tobacco control laws

Research & Evidence Generation

Conducting rigorous research to inform policy decisions and program development across all areas of public health.

  • Epidemiological studies on disease patterns
  • Policy impact assessments
  • Economic analysis of health interventions
  • Community needs assessments
  • Best practice documentation

Alternative Livelihood Programs

Supporting tobacco farmers to transition to sustainable and profitable alternative crops and livelihoods.

  • Farmer training on alternative crops
  • Market linkage development
  • Financial literacy programs
  • Value chain development
  • Cooperative formation support

Policy Advocacy & Development

Influencing health policies at local, national, and international levels through evidence-based advocacy.

  • Legislative drafting support
  • Policy brief development
  • Stakeholder engagement
  • Parliamentary advocacy
  • International policy alignment

Community Engagement & Mobilization

Working directly with communities to address health challenges and build local capacity for sustainable change.

  • Community health education
  • Peer education programs
  • Community health committees
  • Youth engagement initiatives
  • Women's health programs

Capacity Building & Training

Strengthening the skills and knowledge of health professionals, policymakers, and community leaders.

  • Professional training workshops
  • Leadership development programs
  • Technical assistance provision
  • Resource material development
  • Knowledge sharing platforms
